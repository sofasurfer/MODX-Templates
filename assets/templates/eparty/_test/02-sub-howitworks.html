How it works

  1. 1. Choose an e-card amongst all the different categories such as birthday, anniversary, graduation and wedding.
  2. 2. Select a charity where you want your raised funds to go.
  3. 3. Invite your guests by entering details of your party, guest names and email addresses.
  4. 4. Once you hit “send”, your guests will receive the e-card invitation in their email Inbox. As they RSVP, they will indicate whether they wish to donate and if so, they will be led to a PayPal donation gateway where they can pay with their credit cards (no PayPal account needed).
  5. 5. Throughout the entire process, you will be able to track how many guests have replied, how much each have donated, and the total funds raised thus far. Prior to the party, you may send event reminders to guests.
  6. 6. Once all guests have RSVP-ed, Save the Date will send 90% of the total funds raised to your selected charity and a tax-deductible receipt will be sent to you in return.*
  7. 7. After the party, you may send personalized thank you notes to guests indicating how much you have raised together, which charity you donated to, the impact you’ve made, etc.

*The remaining 10% will be contributed towards Save the Date to maintain the cost of operations.

In a nutshell

SavetheDate is your charity-driven, eco-friendly, online party service where all your friends learn the value of giving and receiving while celebrating.
